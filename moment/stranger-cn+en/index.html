Stranger CN+EN

有件事我早想跟你们谈谈了 There’s something I’ve been wanting to talk to you both about. 我知道谈这个很困难，但我非常在乎你们俩 I know this is a difficult conversation,but I care about you both very much. 我也知道你们很在乎彼此 And I know that you care about each other very much. 所以我们一定要立下这些规矩，以便今后建立一个良好环境 And that’s why it’s important that we set these boundaries moving forward,so we can build an environment…. 让我们都能感到自在，被信任和开放的可以分享我们的情绪。 where we all feel comfortable…trusted….and open to share our feelings. 事实上，很久以来，我都忘记那是什么了。 The truth is，for so long, I’d forgotten what those even were. 我一直困在以个地方,可以说困在一个洞里。 I’ve been stuck in one place,in a cave,you might say. 深深的黑暗的洞。 A deep,dark cave. 然后，我在林子里留了些松饼, And then,I left some Eggos out in the woods, 你就走进了我的生活，很久以来，头一次 and you came into my life and …. for the first time in a long time, 我开始有感觉了 I started to feel things again. 我开始感觉快乐了， I started to feel happy. 但近来，我想我觉得与你疏离了 But, lately,I guess I’ve been feeling distant from you. 就好像你，你在远离我. Like you’re,you’re pulling away from me or something. 我想念每晚跟你玩棋盘游戏， I miss playing board games every night, 日出时做三层冰冻松饼大餐 making triple-decker Eggo extravaganzas at sunrise, 睡觉之前 一起看电视 watching westerns together before we doze off. 但我知道你在长大，成长，改变 But I know you’re getting older.Growing,Changing. 我猜 And I guess…. 老实说,这才是让我害怕的 if I’m being really honest,that’s what scares me. 我不希望事情改变 I don’t want things to change. 或许，这才是我来这里的原因, So,I think maybe that’s why I came in here, 想停止那种改变 to try to maybe…stop that change. 逆转时间 To turn back the clock. 让一切回到从前 To make things go back to how they were. 但我知道那是天真 But I know that’s naive.
那不是人生的规律.人生不断向前。 It’s just not how life works.It’s moving. 不管你喜不喜欢，有时时痛苦的。 Always moving,whether you like it or not.And,yeah,something it’s painful. 有时是悲伤的，又有时是令人意外的，快乐的。 Sometimes it’s sad.And sometimes it’s surprising.Happy. 所以你知道吗？ So,you know what? 继续长大把，孩子 Keep on growing up,kid. 别让我拦着你 Don’t let me stop you. 犯错，然后吸取教训 Make mistakes,learn from them. 如果生活伤害了你 因为那是肯定会的，记住那种痛苦 and when life hurts you,because it will,remember the hurt. 那是好事，那意味着你走出了洞， The hurt is good.It means you’re out of that cave. 但求你，如果你不介意，为了你可怜的老爸， But,please,if you don’t mind,for the sake of your poor old dad, 门至少留三英寸 keep the door open three inches.
